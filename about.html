About Me

Web Developer!

I decided to pursuit an undergraduate degree in Computing and Information Technologies because of my strong mathematics background and my love for tech. Now, except attending and performing on my university classes, I try to better equip myself for the working industry by self-learning new technologies and working on projects to build my portfolio.
